Artwork 03

Illustration of a big-bodied black woman against a deep red background, highlighting strength and presence.

Against a passionate crimson backdrop, this piece powerfully celebrates curves, confidence, and unapologetic presence. The subject takes up space boldly and beautifully, challenging narrow beauty standards with every brushstroke. The deep red background amplifies the emotional intensity, creating a sense of warmth, power, and vitality. This artwork is a love letter to body positivity and Black femininity, refusing to diminish or apologize for occupying space. It's both intimate and monumental reminder that all bodies deserve to be seen, celebrated, and immortalized in art. The bold composition speaks to strength found not in conformity, but in embracing one's authentic self.
